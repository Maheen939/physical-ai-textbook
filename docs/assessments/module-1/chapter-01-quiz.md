# Chapter 1 Quiz: Introduction to Physical AI

**Module 1: The Robotic Nervous System** | **Difficulty**: Beginner

---

## Quick Quiz

Test your understanding of Physical AI and embodied intelligence concepts.

### Question 1
What is the main difference between digital AI and Physical AI?

- [ ] Digital AI uses sensors, Physical AI uses cameras
- [ ] Digital AI operates in digital spaces, Physical AI operates in the real world
- [ ] Digital AI is faster than Physical AI
- [ ] Physical AI doesn't use machine learning

### Question 2
Which of the following is a key challenge in Physical AI?

- [ ] Writing code in Python
- [ ] Real-time computation and sensor fusion
- [ ] Creating user interfaces
- [ ] Managing database connections

### Question 3
Why are humanoid robots considered promising for human-centered environments?

- [ ] They are cheaper than other robots
- [ ] They can use human tools and navigate human spaces
- [ ] They don't need batteries
- [ ] They are easier to program

### Question 4
What sensors are commonly used in humanoid robots?

- [ ] Only cameras
- [ ] LIDAR, cameras, IMUs, and force/torque sensors
- [ ] Only touch sensors
- [ ] No sensors needed

### Question 5
What does "embodied intelligence" refer to?

- [ ] AI that has a physical body
- [ ] AI that can speak multiple languages
- [ ] AI that can draw pictures
- [ ] AI that plays chess

---

## Answers

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| 1 | Digital AI operates in digital spaces, Physical AI operates in the real world |
| 2 | Real-time computation and sensor fusion |
| 3 | They can use human tools and navigate human spaces |
| 4 | LIDAR, cameras, IMUs, and force/torque sensors |
| 5 | AI that has a physical body |

## Discussion Points

1. Think of examples where Physical AI differs from digital AI
2. Consider challenges in deploying AI in physical environments
3. Discuss the future of humanoid robots in society
